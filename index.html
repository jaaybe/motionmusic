motion music
your live office
Motion Music is a global production company dedicated to providing unique and fully customized operations solutions to management and label teams in support of artists and their careers in the live entertainment arena. Combining over 20 years of experience in the professional touring industry with a modern approach to world class production solutions, Motion Music provides an unparalleled and singular focus on detail.

Founded in Los Angeles, CA in 2008, Motion Music has since elevated the standard in touring production, partnering with artist’s and their teams to help curate and create events in more than 90 countries. Working with fully established touring entities or helping to bridge the gap for clients expanding into a touring business, Motion Music provides a full service live office to assist managers and labels in helping to achieve their vision and maximize full potential in budgeting, logistics and production management.

From full scale tours, to festivals, television shows and corporate events, Motion Music’s multilingual core team has an unmatched ability to provide realtime access to bottom line operation information through proprietary touring systems. All in an effort to empower artist teams in important every day decision making processes. Maximizing potential and scalability across the full production spectrum is a space where Motion Music thrives.

Alongside an ability to provide Tactical Entertainment Solutions for client teams, Motion Music prides itself in operating at a high level while maintaining a culture of comfort and discretion for clients. 20 years of experience has afforded the Motion team the ability to understand the delicate in’s and out’s of the touring business. Becoming a productive extension to the artist’s team throughout their live business is the highest priority for Motion Music.
